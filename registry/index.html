🌱 Growing to Give

Scatter Good Farm, where we're getting married, is home to Growing to Give — a volunteer-driven nonprofit that grows certified-organic vegetables for donation to families in need and provides farm-based education for youth and the community. The farm has been very generous in hosting this wedding, and a donation in lieu of a gift would mean a great deal to us.

Growing to Give is a 501(c)(3), so donations are tax-deductible.
